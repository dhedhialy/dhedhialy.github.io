Drug-Resistance Forecasting (Kellis Lab · MIT)
May 2026 – Present · XGBoost · AlphaFold · molecular docking
Built a predictive pipeline using XGBoost to forecast unobserved drug-resistant mutations in M. tuberculosis (0.971 AUROC). Integrated population homoplasy data with AlphaFold structural features and drug-pocket coordinates to rank 44,016 variants. Validated against 12,287 CRYPTIC clinical isolates; discovered a literature-novel candidate mechanism (gyrB Q538L). First-author paper in prep.
