> TB Resistance Discovery (Kellis Lab · MIT)

Built a predictive ML pipeline (XGBoost) to forecast unobserved drug-resistant mutations in M. tuberculosis — 0.971 AUROC. Integrated population homoplasy data with AlphaFold structural features and drug-pocket spatial coordinates to rank 44,016 accessible variants. Validated against 12,287 CRYPTIC clinical isolates. Discovered a literature-novel candidate mechanism (gyrB Q538L) via molecular docking.

First-author paper in preparation. github

MIMIC-IV pipeline combining K-Means phenotype discovery with gradient-boosted learning. Cluster-augmented representations hit AUROC 0.869, AUPRC 0.302. First-author paper accepted to IEEE CIBCB 2026.

> BBB-Penetrant Peptide Design (IEEE CIBCB 2026)

Automated in silico pipeline for designing BBB-penetrant peptides targeting EGFRvIII in glioblastoma. Top-ranked cyclized Angiopep-2/GE11 fusion candidate identified through structural prediction, docking, and interface analysis. First-author paper accepted to IEEE CIBCB 2026.
